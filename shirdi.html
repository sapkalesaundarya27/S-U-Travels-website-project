Places To Visit In Shirdi

Shri Sai Baba Samadhi Mandir

The Shirdi Sai Baba Temple is open for visitors from early morning to late at night. Devotees can offer prayers, participate in Aarti (a Hindu religious ritual), and seek the blessings of the saint. The temple also has a museum that displays personal belongings and artifacts of Shirdi Sai Baba, providing insight into his life and teachings.

Apart from managing the Shirdi Sai Baba Temple, the Shirdi Devasthan is also involved in various philanthropic activities, including providing free meals (Prasad) to devotees and running hospitals and schools in the region.

Shri Sai Baba Sansthan Museum

The museum houses a collection of personal belongings and artifacts of Shirdi Sai Baba, including his clothes, utensils, footwear, and other items that he used during his lifetime. It also has a collection of photographs and paintings that depict the life and teachings of the saint.

The Shri Sai Baba Sansthan Museum is a popular tourist destination in Shirdi and provides visitors with insight into the life and legacy of the saint. It is open from 9 am to 6 pm every day, except for Thursdays when it is closed.
